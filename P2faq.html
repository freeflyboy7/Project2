What can should I bring?
Gear—$10,000 Everest is not the place to skimp on the grade of your down jacket. Buying a cheaper featherweight on your summit mittens might cost you a hand to frostbite. I never knew gloves could cost $300 or that I would own two pairs of them.
What can I expect when I get there?
An Everest climb starts at around $35,000 because mountain permits begin at $10,000 (the price varies based on the number in the expedition). In addition, a virtual village of Sherpas—porters, cooks, and guides—is required to get you to the top. Fo $3,500 airplane ticket to get to Nepal, depending on whether you fly first class or coach.
Will there be help in case of emergency?
A Sherpa takes home only $3,500 total for the climb. I treated mine to two yaks, which he named “Philip” and “Roth” after I introduced him to Philip Roth novels. You also have to purchase medical evacuation insurance, which starts at $500.
